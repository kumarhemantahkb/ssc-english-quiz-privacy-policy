Privacy Policy

For SSC English Quiz MCQ Application

Effective Date: May 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at SSC English Quiz MCQ ("App", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application and its associated services.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal identity information (such as your name, address, or phone number) directly. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, improve the app, and serve relevant advertisements.

2. Third-Party Services & Data Collection

Our App utilizes the following third-party services. Please review their privacy policies to understand how they handle data:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app functionality and updates. (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies to show personalized or non-personalized ads. (Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Analytics & Firestore: Used to track app usage, crashes, and fetch quiz data to provide you with a seamless experience. (Privacy Policy)

3. Local Storage (Device Data)

To enhance your learning experience, the App saves your test history, quiz scores, and "Mistake Bucket" locally on your device (using SharedPreferences/Local Cache). This data remains on your phone and is not uploaded or shared with our servers.

4. Use of Your Information

The anonymous data collected through third-party tools is used to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our App.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance and content.
  • Deliver targeted advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Fix bugs and technical issues.

5. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

6. Security of Data

We value your trust in providing us with your information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
